## WEEKLY CONTENT CALENDAR

### Monday - Market Intelligence Day
**Time Block:** 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

| Task | Business | Output | Platform |
|------|----------|--------|----------|
| Draft BBGV Newsletter | BBGV | Market intel roundup | Email (180K list) |
| GunStreamer Launch Thread | GunStreamer | Launch narrative, FFL value prop | Twitter/X |
| Rentagun Product Spotlight | Rentagun | Featured rental + use case | Instagram + Facebook |

**BBGV Newsletter Requirements:**
- **Length:** 400-600 words
- **Format:** 3-5 market insights, 1 featured valuation, 1 CTA
- **CTA:** Rotate between: Niche book promotion, InstaCash offer, Valuation signup
- **Deadline:** Draft by 11 AM, review/schedule for Tuesday 8 AM send

**GunStreamer Thread Requirements:**
- **Length:** 5-8 tweets
- **Hook:** Problem (trust in online gun sales) → Solution (live commerce)
- **Include:** FFL success story or buyer testimonial (if available)

**Rentagun Post Requirements:**
- **Format:** Hero image of featured firearm + caption
- **Caption Structure:** Gun name → Use case → Rental tier → CTA
- **Example:** "Desert Eagle .50 AE - Bachelor party? Bucket list? This is your gun. Available in Pro Tier ($199/mo). Try before you buy. [Link]"

| Task | Business | Output | Platform |
|------|----------|--------|----------|
| Send BBGV Newsletter | BBGV | Market intel email | Klaviyo |
| Publish GunStreamer Thread | GunStreamer | Launch narrative | Twitter/X |
| BBGV Blog Post | BBGV | SEO-optimized article | Website |

**BBGV Blog Post Requirements:**
- **Length:** 1200-1500 words
- **Topics:** Rotate through:

- **SEO:** Target 1 primary keyword, 3-5 related keywords
- **CTAs:** Newsletter signup, Free valuation, Niche book mention
