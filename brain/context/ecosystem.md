# The Ecosystem Synergy

Every business feeds the others. BBGV's 180K email list is the launch asset for Rentagun.

```
BBGV (Lead Gen) ─────────────────────────────────────┐
  │ "What's my gun worth?"                           │
  │     ↓                                            │
  │ Inheritor/Seller → InstaCash Offer or Consign   │
  │     ↓                                            │
  └──────────────→ GunStreamer (Sell Platform) ←────┘
                        ↓
              Buyer watches stream
                        ↓
              "I want to try that first"
                        ↓
                    Rentagun (Try)
                        ↓
              "I want to buy"
                        ↓
                    Egunco (Fulfillment)
```

## Flow Details

### BBGV → Everything
- 180K email list = distribution channel for all products
- Inheritor content brings sellers
- Sellers become consigners → GunStreamer inventory
- Collectors rent before expanding collections → Rentagun

### GunStreamer → Rentagun
- Buyer sees gun on stream, wants to try it first
- Rentagun provides the "try before you buy" experience
- Reduces buyer hesitation on expensive firearms

### Rentagun → Egunco
- Customer rents, decides to buy
- Rental fee applies to purchase price
- Egunco handles retail fulfillment

### Vault Service (Future)
- BBGV collectors store their collections
- Rentagun rents out vault guns for revenue
- Full circle: value → store → rent → sell
