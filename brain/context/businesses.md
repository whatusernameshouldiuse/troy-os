## 1. BBGV (Blue Book of Gun Values)

**Website:** bluebookofgunvalues.com
**What:** 45+ year firearms valuation authority - the "Kelley Blue Book" for guns
**Tech Stack:** Laravel (web app), Subscription SaaS
**Current Status:** Transitioning from paid-only to freemium model

### Business Model (2026 Direction)
- Primary Revenue: Niche books (manufacturer-specific, era-specific like "Great War to Cold War")
- InstaCash Offers: Buy guns directly from inheritors/sellers
- Consignment: Vault guns, sell through GunStreamer
- Tiered Plans: Free (lead gen), $10 Power User, $30 FFL
- Future: Vault service for collectors (storage + rental via Rentagun)

### Key Features
- 180K+ gun valuations
- Price history database
- Authentication/grading service (PSA-style)
- 180K email subscriber list

### ICPs
- **Inheritors:** "I inherited grandpa's guns, what are they worth?"
- **Collectors:** Serious valuation needs, collection tracking
- **FFLs:** Wholesale pricing, inventory valuation
- **Insurance/Appraisers:** Professional valuation services

### Content Priorities
- Inheritor journey content ("What to do when you inherit guns")
- Newsletter (weekly market intel)
- Book production pipeline
- InstaCash landing pages

## 2. GunStreamer

**Website:** gunstreamer.com
**What:** Live commerce platform for firearms - "Whatnot for guns"
**Tech Stack:** Custom platform for FFL live streaming
**Status:** Launching/Pre-launch

### Business Model
- SaaS for FFLs to stream inventory sales
- 3% transaction fees
- Fed by BBGV consignment inventory
- Licensed FFLs only

### Positioning
- NOT a YouTube alternative anymore
- Live commerce solves the TRUST problem in online gun sales
- See it on camera, ask questions, watch it cycle, THEN decide

### Content Priorities
- Twitter launch threads
- FFL onboarding content
- Buyer landing page
- Trust-building content (scam stories, verification value)

## 3. Rentagun

**Website:** rentagun.com
**What:** First national try-before-you-buy firearm rental service
**Tech Stack:** WordPress + WooCommerce + WooCommerce Bookings
**Status:** Active, launched at SHOT Show 2026 (Jan 26-29)

### Business Model
- Basic Tier: $99/month - 1 rental/month, 7 days, $1K MSRP max
- Pro Tier: $199/month - 2 rentals/month, 7 days each, $3K MSRP max
- Rental fees apply to purchase
- Ships to FFL, customer completes background check

### Inventory
~216 firearms (150+ available)
- Desert Eagle (.50 AE)
- Colt Python (.357 Magnum)
- S&W Model 500
- HK MP5 (.22 LR)
- Steyr AUG
- Plus 140+ handguns, rifles, shotguns

### ICPs
1. **The Considerer:** Wants to buy but unsure which model
2. **The Experience Seeker:** Bachelor parties, bucket list (Thompson, etc.)
3. **The Hunter:** Needs specialty rifle for one hunt
4. **The New Gun Owner:** First purchase, overwhelmed by options

### Brand Voice
- Straight-shooter, honest, no BS
- "Try before you buy"
- "Stop researching, start shooting"
- "Rent it Saturday, decide Sunday"
- **BANNED:** "Netflix of guns", political statements

### Technical
- RevenueHunt quiz for product matching
- 7-day advance booking
- In-person pickup (Birmingham area)
- Excludes California + 12 other restrictive states
