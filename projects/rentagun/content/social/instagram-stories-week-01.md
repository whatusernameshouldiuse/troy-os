## Production Notes

### Story Design Guidelines
- Use Rentagun Red (#E63946) as accent
- Black text on white background OR white text on dark photos
- Keep text minimal - stories are for quick consumption
- Use native Instagram fonts for authenticity

### Posting Times
- Post stories between 8 AM - 10 PM local time
- Space 2-3 hours between story slides
- Use "Add yours" stickers to encourage engagement

### Engagement Protocol
- Respond to all DMs within 24 hours
- Reply to question box responses
- Reshare poll results the next day

### Tracking
- Check story insights weekly
- Note which story types get most taps/replies
- Adjust content mix based on data
