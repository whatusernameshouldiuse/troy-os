## Business Context

**What:** 45+ year firearms valuation authority - the "Kelley Blue Book" for guns

### Business Model (2026 Direction)
- Primary Revenue: Niche books (manufacturer-specific, era-specific)
- InstaCash Offers: Buy guns directly from inheritors/sellers
- Consignment: Vault guns, sell through GunStreamer
- Tiered Plans: Free (lead gen), $10 Power User, $30 FFL
- Future: Vault service for collectors

### Key Assets
- 180K+ gun valuations
- Price history database
- Authentication/grading service (PSA-style)
- **180K email subscriber list** (key launch asset)
