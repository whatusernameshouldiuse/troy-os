## Situation Summary

GunStreamer is live. FFLs are streaming. The platform solves a real problem (online gun buying has a trust deficit) and the first wave of emails has shipped. The 184K-contact list is the biggest asset in the building — but most of those contacts came from BBGV cross-pollination and EGunCo data, not organic GunStreamer interest. They need to be educated, converted, and segmented before the real growth machine can be activated.

**Current state:**
- Wave 1 sent (10K profiles, 3 sub-batches)
- 17 waves remaining, 174K profiles + 135K still in Reoon validation queue
- No regular newsletter cadence running
- Twitter/social is active but not systematic
- FFL onboarding content needs a permanent home
- Zero SEO presence (domain is new)

**Three-phase strategy:**
1. **Activate** (now → 60 days): Complete the 184K email launch wave. Convert contacts into buyers and FFL applicants. Build the two-sided foundation.
2. **Build** (60–120 days): Launch recurring email cadence for both audiences. Publish foundational SEO content. Build FFL onboarding machine.
3. **Scale** (120+ days): Paid FFL acquisition. Buyer growth through SEO + BBGV cross-sell. Live commerce content compounding.

---

## The Two-Sided Growth Loop

GunStreamer's growth is a flywheel. Every piece of marketing needs to feed it:

```
More FFLs streaming
        ↓
More live inventory
        ↓
Better buyer experience
        ↓
More buyer signups + views
        ↓
More FFL revenue
        ↓
More FFLs streaming
```

**Implication:** FFL acquisition is always the constraint. Without supply, buyer demand has nowhere to go. FFL content and outreach is always the higher priority channel.

## Phase 3: Acquisition Scale (Weeks 12+)

### FFL Acquisition — Outbound Playbook

**Target:** 100 FFLs streaming within 6 months of full launch

**Channel 1 — LinkedIn Outreach**
FFL owners and gun shop managers are on LinkedIn. They respond to direct, professional pitches.
- Target title: "Owner," "FFL Dealer," "Firearms Dealer"
- Opening: share one relevant FFL win story, not a feature pitch
- Sequence: connect → send win story → follow-up with "would this work for your inventory?"
- Delegate to VA when volume exceeds 20 outreaches/week

**Channel 2 — GunBroker + Armslist Dealers**
Dealers who are already selling online are primed — they've solved the "trust the internet" objection.
- Identify high-volume dealers on GunBroker
- Reach out with: "You're already doing this online. Adding live streaming takes 15 minutes to set up and expands your reach."

**Channel 3 — Gun Show Circuit**
FFLs who do gun shows are natural streamers — they already demo guns in person. Live streaming is just doing that on camera.
- Gun show calendar: identify regional shows in TX, AZ, TN, KY, WY (high FFL density)
- Create a printed one-pager for show outreach (Troy to distribute or hire VA)

**Channel 4 — BBGV FFL Database**
BBGV has an FFL segment in its Klaviyo list. Once deliverability is recovered, a dedicated outreach sequence targeting FFLs specifically is the highest-quality outreach channel available.
- Segment: BBGV FFL profiles (coordinate with BBGV Phase 2 timeline)
- Message: "You're already using BBGV to price inventory. Now stream it to buyers nationwide."

### Buyer Acquisition — Paid Channels

**Facebook/Instagram (Primary Paid)**
- Target: gun buyers 25–55, interests in firearms, shooting sports, hunting, collecting
- Creative: short video (15–30 sec) showing a live stream — buyer asking a question, FFL answering on camera, sale completing
- Hook: "Before you buy another gun online, watch this" (leads to stream highlight)
- Landing page: buyer signup with "notify me when [gun type] goes live" opt-in
- Budget: start $500/month test, scale if CAC < $8

**YouTube Pre-Roll (Secondary Paid)**
- Target: gun review channels, FFL YouTube channels, firearms education content
- Creative: 15-sec non-skippable "This is what buying a gun live looks like" with platform demo
- Goal: awareness + signups

**BBGV Email (Organic — Highest Quality)**
- Timeline: activate once BBGV Phase 1 is complete
- Value: pre-qualified gun buyers who already trust Blue Book
- Expected conversion: higher than cold paid traffic

## Voice Guardrails (Quick Reference)

**Every piece of GunStreamer content must pass these:**

1. **Straight Shooter test:** Could Troy say this to an FFL owner without sounding like a startup pitch? No buzzwords.
2. **Trust test:** Are we showing, not telling? (Specific example > vague claim)
3. **No ban list violations:** No "revolutionary," "disruptive," "game-changing." No political content. No attacks on GunBroker/Armslist.
4. **Dual-audience check:** Does this serve FFLs, buyers, or both? Content should be clearly for one.
5. **CTA clarity:** One CTA per email/post. Either "apply to stream" (FFL) or "watch a stream" (buyer). Never both in the same piece.
