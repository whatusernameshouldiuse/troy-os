## Brand Voice & Positioning

**Core Message:** "The future of gun buying is live."

**Voice Characteristics:**
- Exciting but professional
- Opportunity-focused (not desperate)
- Trust-building (addresses scam problem directly)
- Community-oriented (FFLs and buyers together)

**Positioning Statement:**
"GunStreamer is the first live commerce platform built exclusively for the firearms industry. Licensed FFLs stream their inventory live, buyers watch guns being handled and demonstrated in real-time, then purchase with confidence. No more grainy photos. No more scams. Just transparent, live gun buying."

**DO Say:**
- "Watch it cycle before you buy"
- "See every scratch, ask every question"
- "Live transparency builds trust"
- "Your FFL, now nationwide"
- "The Whatnot for guns"

**DON'T Say:**
- "YouTube alternative" (we're commerce, not content)
- Political statements
- "Disrupt the industry" (we're building it)
- Anything that sounds like we're circumventing regulations

### Launch Readiness Indicators

**Green Light Indicators (Ready for Full Launch):**

   - ‚úÖ 20+ FFLs streaming regularly
   - ‚úÖ 80%+ retention (FFLs stream 2+ times/week)
   - ‚úÖ FFLs in 10+ states
   - ‚úÖ Average 4+ guns sold per stream

   - ‚úÖ 500+ on waitlist
   - ‚úÖ 50+ buyers watching per stream
   - ‚úÖ 10%+ viewer ‚Üí buyer conversion
   - ‚úÖ 20%+ repeat buyer rate

3. **Platform Quality**
   - ‚úÖ <2% "not as described" complaints
   - ‚úÖ <5% refund rate
   - ‚úÖ Stream uptime >99%
   - ‚úÖ Payment processing smooth (no major issues)

4. **Unit Economics**
   - ‚úÖ 3% transaction fee covers payment processing + platform costs
   - ‚úÖ Average sale price >$800 (healthy margins for everyone)
   - ‚úÖ CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) < $50 for buyers
   - ‚úÖ FFLs profitable within first month

   - ‚úÖ 10+ FFL testimonials collected
   - ‚úÖ 20+ buyer testimonials collected
   - ‚úÖ 3+ case studies published
   - ‚úÖ Zero major complaints publicly visible

   - ‚úÖ 1,000+ Twitter followers
   - ‚úÖ 3%+ engagement rate on content
   - ‚úÖ Landing page converts at 15%+
   - ‚úÖ Email sequences tested and optimized

- ‚ùå FFL churn >15% (product-market fit issue)

- ‚ùå <5% viewer ‚Üí buyer conversion (platform UX issue)

- ‚ùå FFLs not streaming regularly (engagement issue)
