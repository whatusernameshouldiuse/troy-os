## Quality Standards

- **Unique content:** 50% minimum, 60%+ target
- **Word count:** 1200+ words
- **Neighborhoods:** 8-12 real, verified on Google Maps
- **Landmarks:** 3-5 with actual addresses
- **No generic phrases:** "downtown", "residential area"

---

## Production System

- Cursor Deep Research for city profiles
- Python generators for location pages
- 50-60% unique content minimum
- Quality validation before scale
